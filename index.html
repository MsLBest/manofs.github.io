About Us

Mano Financial Services (MFS) is the parent company of ManoFX, a revolutionary platform digitizing foreign exchange under Liberia's dual currency regime. We’re building secure and transparent financial infrastructure for Africa’s future.

Our Product: ManoFX

ManoFX provides a digital marketplace for foreign exchange traders, businesses, and individuals to transact seamlessly across Liberia and beyond. It is monitored by the Central Bank and bridges mobile money with digital FX trade.
